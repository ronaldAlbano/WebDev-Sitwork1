The Purple House - Home of the Best Lugaw in Town

Discover the savory goodness of our signature Lugaw

Our Lugaw is a hot, comforting bowl of rice porridge, with toppings of your choice. It's perfect for breakfast, lunch, and dinner! You can choose from a variety of toppings, including chicken, beef, pork, or mixed seafood. Each bowl is served with a boiled egg, green onions, and fried garlic. Our secret recipe ensures that each bite is rich and flavorful.

Try our other delicious Filipino dishes

We also offer a wide selection of Filipino dishes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. From adobo to sinigang, we have it all. Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients and traditional cooking methods to bring out the authentic flavors of each dish. Come visit us and taste the best of Filipino cuisine!
